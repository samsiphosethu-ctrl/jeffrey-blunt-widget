THCa Flower
The Backbone

The foundation of every Jeffrey. We packed it with a Face Mints × Biscotti × Sherbert hybrid — a complex, terpene-rich flower with earthy depth and notes of pine and sweet cream. It's what gives the Jeffrey its structure and staying power.

🍯
Live Rosin
The Amplifier

Solventless, small-batch, and seriously potent. Live rosin is extracted from fresh-frozen flower at low temps to preserve the full terpene profile — giving every hit a rich, layered flavor that regular infused pre-rolls can't touch.

💎
THCa Diamond Dust
The Finisher

Crystalline THCa concentrate ground into a fine dust and coated across the blunt. Diamonds are among the purest forms of THCa you can find — and that coating means every section of the Jeffrey burns with extra intensity, from first light to final ash.
